Mean Height

The bar graph represents the mean height of children of age under five years in states surveyed under the CAB survey. The state of Assam has the highest value for mean height whereas Uttar Pradesh registers the lowest average height. We can also infer that males have greater average height than females in most of the states. Height of a child indicates the nutrition received by a child which affects physical growth.

Mean Weight

The bar graph represents the mean weight of children of age under five years in states surveyed under the CAB survey. Approximately all the states have similar values for weight, which is a good indicator. We can also infer that males have greater average weight than females in all of the states.

Haemoglobin Level

The histogram shows the average haemoglobin level in children of age under five years in states surveyed under the CAB survey. The states of Odisha and Chhattisgarh have the highest average haemoglobin levels followed by Assam. Haemoglobin is a direct measure of Iron content in diet.

Month when first Breast fed:

The graph shows the time when the children were first breastfed. Mother's milk is the most important food for an infant and it can be related to various health factors. Breast feeding within one hour of birth is the healthiest thing to do. We see that Chhattisgarh, Odisha and Madhya Pradesh are the only states who register 80% when it comes to breast feeding within an hour. The proportion is very discouraging in the states of Rajasthan and Uttar Pradesh with almost 50%. Jharkhand has a disturbing proportion of infants who have never been breastfed with almost 1%.

Total Breast Feeding

We see that almost 60% of the infants are breast fed within an hour of birth. The proportion has to be increased by spreading awareness among people.


Month when Water was first fed:

The histogram shows the average age of infants in months when they were first given water. According to the WHO, an infant should be fed water after he/she is 6 months old. As we can see that all the states have an average age of less than 6 months with Uttar Pradesh dropping below three. Awareness regarding intake of water should be spread for good health of children.

Month when Animal Milk was first fed:

The histogram shows the average age of infants in months when they were first given animal's milk. It is advised that a baby should be almost a year old to be given animal's milk. Mother's milk is the most important nutrition source for the baby till that age. We can see that the infographics are discouraging which indicates a lack of awareness among the public.

Month when Semi Solid Food was first fed:

The histogram shows the average age of infants in months when they were first given semi solid food. It is advised that a baby should be almost six months old to be given semi solid food. We can see that three states - Odisha, Chhattisgarh and Madhya Pradesh don't reach the standards. General awareness needs to spread.

Month when Solid Food was first fed:

The histogram shows the average age of infants in months when they were first given solid food. It is advised that a baby should be almost 7 - 8 months old to be given solid food. We can see that only Uttarakhand and Bihar have proper statistics for intake of solid food.

Month when Vegetables were first fed:

The histogram shows the average age of infants in months when they were first given vegetables. It is advised that a baby should be almost 7 - 8 months old to be given vegetables. States of Uttarakhand, Bihar and Assam have good statistics.
